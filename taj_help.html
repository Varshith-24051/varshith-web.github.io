Frequently Asked Questions

How do I make a reservation?

You can easily make a reservation through our website, mobile app, or by contacting our dedicated reservations team. Visit the "Book Now" section on our website or call us at [Phone Number].

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability.

What are the cancellation policies?

Cancellation policies vary depending on the rate and type of reservation. Please refer to your booking confirmation for specific details regarding cancellations and any applicable fees.

Account & Membership

How do I manage my Taj InnerCircle membership?

You can manage your Taj InnerCircle account by logging in on our website or app. Here, you can view your points, tier status, and exclusive offers.

I've forgotten my login details. What should I do?

If you've forgotten your password, please click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it. For your username, please contact our support team.
