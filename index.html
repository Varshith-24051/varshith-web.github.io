Kerala

Kerala is a tropical paradise in southern India, known for its breathtaking landscapes, rich culture, and serene backwaters. Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or a taste of India's unique traditions, Kerala has something for everyone. From the lush hill stations to pristine beaches, and Ayurvedic retreats to vibrant festivals, this enchanting state offers a perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences.

Our Stays in Kerala

Emerald Canopy Suite
Discover serenity and spiritual connection in the Sacred Grove Retreat, inspired by the ancient groves revered in Kerala. This tranquil room is designed to promote relaxation and rejuvenation, featuring natural stone accents, calming earth tones, and a meditation area.
Monsoon Mist Chalet
Enveloped by the emerald embrace of Kerala's rainforest, the Monsoon Mist Chalet offers a secluded haven. Awaken to the gentle patter of rain on the lush canopy and the invigorating aroma of wet earth.
Tribal Echo Chamber
Nestled where the rainforest meets the serene backwaters of Kerala, the Emerald Backwater Bungalow offers a harmonious blend of natural beauty. Wake to the gentle lapping of water and the calls of waterbirds.
